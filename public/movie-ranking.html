The Best Movies According to Kirwin

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

Spirited Away is a Japanese animated film that was released in 2001. It was written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli.

Ex Machina

Ex Machina is a science fiction film that was released in 2014. It was written and directed by Alex Garland and produced by DNA Films.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a superhero film that was released in 2008. It was directed by Christopher Nolan and produced by Warner Bros. Pictures.
